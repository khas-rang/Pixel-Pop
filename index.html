About

Crafting Joy
through Games

We are a casual gaming company based in Tripura, India. Our mission is to create simple yet engaging games that can be enjoyed by players of all ages. Our flagship game, Pixel Pop, challenges players to destroy and pop pixels to clear the board and advance through levels.
Our team is dedicated to providing fun and entertainment through innovative game design and user-friendly interfaces. We believe that games should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to make our games as enjoyable and inclusive as possible.
